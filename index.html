Trench Grade

Everything is a laser reading, measured down from the beam. Bigger number = lower.

1Ground readings along the line

Set the laser up anywhere it can see the whole run. Walk the trench line with the tape: at each point, enter the distance from the start and the reading from the beam down to the ground. Bigger reading = lower ground. The first point (distance 0) is your station at the house.
